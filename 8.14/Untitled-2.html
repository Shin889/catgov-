Governors of Catanduanes

THIRD PHILIPPINE REPUBLIC (1945–1981)
1

Remigio Socito

September 26, 1945 - December 30, 1947
Origin: Virac
Last Lieutenant governor of subprovince of Catanduanes under Albay. Appointed first governor after Catanduanes became a separate province.
2

Alfonso Vera Usero

December 30, 1947 - December 30, 1951
Origin: Viga
First elected governor of Catanduanes. Served one term and established the foundation for democratic governance in the province.
3

Juan Molina Alberto

December 30, 1951 - December 30, 1959
Origin: Virac
Elected twice, serving two consecutive terms. Known for his dedication to public service and provincial development initiatives.
4

Jorge Vera Almojuela

December 30, 1959 - December 30, 1967
Origin: Pandan
Elected twice and served two consecutive terms. Focused on infrastructure development and connecting the northern municipalities to the capital.
5

Vicente Molina Alberto (Longest Serving)

December 30, 1967 - March 15, 1986
Origin: Virac
Longest serving governor of Catanduanes. Elected in 3 consecutive terms and continued serving through the Fourth Republic until 1986.
FIFTH PHILIPPINE REPUBLIC (1986–present)
6

Leandro I. Verceles Sr.

March 16, 1986 - June 30, 1992
Origin: Virac
Former ambassador. Appointed by President Corazon C. Aquino in 1986, then elected to continue serving until 1992.
7

Rosalie Alberto-Estacio

June 30, 1992 - June 30, 1995
Origin: Virac
First female governor of Catanduanes. Broke barriers and paved the way for women's leadership in provincial governance.
8

Severo C. Alcantara

June 30, 1995 - June 30, 1998
Origin: Virac
Focused on infrastructure development and modernization initiatives during his single term as governor.
9

Hector S. Sanchez

June 30, 1998 - June 30, 2001
Origin: Virac
Served one term with focus on economic development and improving provincial services for the people of Catanduanes.
10

Leandro B. Verceles Jr.

June 30, 2001 - June 30, 2007
Origin: Virac
Son of former governor Leandro I. Verceles Sr. Elected twice and served two consecutive terms focusing on modernization.
11

Joseph Chua Cua (Non-consecutive terms)

2007-2013, 2016-2025
Origin: San Andres
Served two separate periods as governor. First elected twice (2007-2013), then returned for three consecutive terms (2016-2025).
12

Araceli B. Wong

June 30, 2013 - June 30, 2016
Origin: Pandan
Second female governor of Catanduanes. Served one term between Joseph Cua's non-consecutive gubernatorial periods.

Patrick Alain T. Azanza (Current)

June 30, 2025 - Present
Origin: Virac
Current governor of Catanduanes. Recently took office in 2025, bringing new leadership and vision for the province's future development.
