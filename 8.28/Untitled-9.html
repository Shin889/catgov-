PESO complied with legal mandates and good governance by using funds for programs under RA 8759 (as amended by RA 10691) and DILG requirements, with confirmed and validated leadership ensuring operational integrity. The Provincial Government of Catanduanes supported PESO by providing the necessary budget and office facilities.

Under the IRR of the PESO Act of 1999, as amended by RA No. 10691, an institutionalized PESO must have permanent plantilla positions and budget allocations for personnel, operations, and capital outlay. It must also have designated office space to provide employment facilitation services and serve as an information center for other DOLE programs.
