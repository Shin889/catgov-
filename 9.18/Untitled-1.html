Message From The Governor

Patrick Alain T. Azanza

Governor

Good day, fellow Catandunganons!

I am deeply honored and grateful for the trust you have placed in me to serve as your Governor. This victory is not mine alone—it belongs to all of us who believe in a brighter future for Catanduanes. Having dedicated my life to honest service, education, and helping the marginalized, I am proud to call this province my home—a land rich in hardworking farmers, fisherfolk, and youth who deserve a government that listens and acts for their welfare.

As your Governor, I pledge to uphold the values we share: honest service, people-centered programs, care for our environment, and equal opportunities for all. Let us unite in hope and action to build a Catanduanes we can all be proud of, where traditions are honored and every voice is heard.

Thank you for your trust. Let’s work hand in hand for the future of our beloved Catanduanes! Mabuhay!

SOCIAL MEDIA

Patrick Azanza
